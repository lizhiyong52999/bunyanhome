Who We Are

Bunyan Home is a professional B2B supplier specializing in high-quality bathroom and home fixtures. Established in 2018 and based in Guangzhou, Guangdong, China, we have grown to serve 30+ countries across the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and Southeast Asia.

We operate on a factory-direct model — no middlemen, no markup. Our manufacturing partners are carefully selected and audited to ensure compliance with international quality standards.
