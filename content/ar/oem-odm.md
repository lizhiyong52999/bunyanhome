## OEM/ODM Services

Bunyan Home offers comprehensive OEM and ODM manufacturing services for bathroom and home fixtures. We work with distributors, brands, and project buyers to create custom products tailored to your market needs.

### What We Offer
- **Custom Logo & Branding** — Laser engraving, embossing, printed labels
- **Custom Packaging** — Branded boxes, inserts, barcode printing
- **Custom Finishes** — Chrome, Brushed Nickel, Matte Black, Brushed Gold, Gun Grey, PVD coatings
- **Custom Design** — Product modification or new product development
- **Small Batch Orders** — MOQ from 50 pieces for trial orders

### OEM Process
1. **Inquiry** — Send us your requirements, drawings, or samples
2. **Quotation** — We provide detailed pricing within 24 hours
3. **Sample** — Prototype production and approval (7-15 days)
4. **Production** — Mass production (15-30 days depending on quantity)
5. **Quality Control** — 100% inspection before shipment
6. **Shipping** — FOB Guangzhou/Shenzhen or CIF your port

### Certifications & Compliance
- CE certification for European markets
- ISO9001 quality management system
- SABER certification for Saudi Arabia
- ESMA standards for UAE market
