## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is your minimum order quantity?
Our standard MOQ is 50 pieces per product. For customized orders, MOQ may vary depending on the level of customization.

### Do you offer samples?
Yes, samples are available for most products. Sample fees are refundable upon bulk order placement.

### What are your payment terms?
Standard terms are T/T 30% deposit, 70% balance before shipment. L/C at sight is available for qualified buyers.

### How long is your production lead time?
Production typically takes 15-25 days after order confirmation, depending on order size and customization requirements.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide. We offer FOB, CIF, and DDP terms. Our primary shipping methods include sea freight, air freight, and express courier.
