## Our Story

Bunyan Home was established with a clear mission: to provide global B2B buyers with direct access to high-quality bathroom and home fixtures at competitive factory prices.

With over 5 years of experience in manufacturing and exporting, we have built strong relationships with factories in China and established our headquarters in Guangzhou to better serve our Middle East and global clients.

## Why Choose Us

### Factory Direct Pricing
By working directly with manufacturers and maintaining minimal overhead, we offer pricing that eliminates middleman markups. Our clients consistently report savings of 20-40% compared to traditional suppliers.

### Quality Assurance
All our products undergo strict quality control at every stage of production. We hold CE, ISO9001, SABER, and ESMA certifications, ensuring compliance with international standards.

### OEM/ODM Capabilities
From custom logos and packaging to complete product customization, our experienced team can bring your vision to life. We've successfully completed hundreds of OEM projects for clients worldwide.

### Global Logistics
With warehouses in China and efficient shipping partnerships, we deliver to over 30 countries. Whether you need FOB, CIF, or DDP terms, we have flexible options to meet your needs.
