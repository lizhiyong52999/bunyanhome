Factory Direct — No Middleman

Bunyan Home is a professional B2B supplier specializing in high-quality bathroom and home fixtures since 2018. Based in Guangzhou, China, we serve distributors, contractors, hotels, and property developers across 30+ countries worldwide.

We offer factory-direct pricing, OEM/ODM customization, and flexible shipping options (FOB, CIF, DDP). Every product is manufactured to meet international standards — CE, ISO9001, SABER, ESMA, and SASO certified.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for B2B buyers

What is your MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?
Our standard MOQ is 50 pieces per SKU. For trial orders, we can negotiate smaller quantities. OEM/ODM orders may have higher MOQ depending on customization requirements.
What certifications do your products have?
All our products meet CE, ISO9001, SABER (Saudi Arabia), ESMA (UAE), and SASO standards. We can provide compliance certificates upon request.
What are your production and shipping lead times?
Sample lead time: 7-10 days. Production lead time: 15-25 days depending on order volume. Shipping: express (3-5 days), air freight (5-7 days), sea freight (15-30 days).
Do you offer OEM/ODM services?
Yes. We offer custom logo, packaging, color, and design. Contact our sales team with your requirements for a tailored quote.
What materials are used in your products?
Our products use premium materials: brass (CW617N), 304 stainless steel, ABS engineering plastics, and tempered glass for mirrors. All materials comply with international standards.
What payment terms do you accept?
We accept T/T (30% deposit, 70% before shipment), L/C at sight, and PayPal for sample orders. We also support secure trade assurance.
