Frequently Asked Questions

Everything B2B buyers need to know before ordering

What is your MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?
Standard MOQ is 50 pieces per SKU. For trial orders, we can negotiate smaller quantities. OEM/ODM orders may have higher MOQ depending on customization.
What certifications do your products have?
All our products meet CE, ISO9001, SABER (Saudi Arabia), ESMA (UAE), and SASO standards. We can provide compliance certificates upon request.
What are your production and shipping lead times?
Sample: 7-10 days. Production: 15-25 days. Shipping: Express (3-5 days), Air freight (5-7 days), Sea freight (15-30 days).
Do you offer OEM/ODM services?
Yes. We offer custom logo, packaging, color, and design. Contact our sales team with your requirements for a tailored quote.
What materials are used in your products?
Brass (CW617N), 304 stainless steel, ABS engineering plastics, tempered glass for mirrors. All materials comply with international standards.
What payment terms do you accept?
T/T (30% deposit, 70% before shipment), L/C at sight, and PayPal for sample orders. We also support trade assurance.
Can I get samples before bulk order?
Yes, samples are available. Sample lead time is 7-10 days. Sample cost is refundable upon bulk order.
How do you handle quality control?
We have a 3-stage QC process: incoming material inspection, in-process inspection, and pre-shipment inspection. All products are tested to meet international standards.
